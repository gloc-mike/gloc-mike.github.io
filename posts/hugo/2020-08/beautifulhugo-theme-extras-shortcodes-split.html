Beautifulhugo Theme Extras - Shortcodes (Split)

Published: Thu 13 August 2020
By Michael B

In Hugo.

tags: hugo beautifulhugo shortcodes

Another extra beautifulhugo theme shortcode is called split. This shortcode splits the page into two columns. The example shown in the doco in the beautifulhugo repo (at the time of writing) was a little confusing because the closing endcolumns tag was incorrect (missing an s). Got there in the end. 😅

It should look like this:

{{</* columns */>}}
    First column on the right side of the page
{{</* column */>}}
    Second column on the left side of the page
{{</* endcolumns */>}}

Yes, the first tag is columns whereas the intermediate tag is column. And the closing tag is again a plural i.e. endcolumns.
